You cannot get Carer’s Allowance if the person you care for is not getting a qualifying disability benefit

You can only get Carer’s Allowance if the person you provide care for is getting one of the listed disability benefits. If they have applied for one, you must wait until they get a decision before applying for Carer’s Allowance.

Other support available

You may be able to apply for Carer’s Credit instead, even if the person you provide care for does not get one of the listed disability benefits.

To check if there is anything else you are entitled to, or for advice and support, you can contact the Make the Call service.
